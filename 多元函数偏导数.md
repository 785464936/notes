# 多元函数偏导数

## 偏导数的定义

$$
f^{\prime}(x_{0},y_{0}) = \lim_{ \Delta \to 0 } \dfrac{f(x_{0}+\Delta x,y_{0})-f(x_{0},y_{0})}{\Delta x}
$$

> [!tip]
> $\displaystyle \left. \frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x } \right|_{\substack{x=x_{0}\\ y=y_{0}}}$ 与 $f^{\prime}_{1}(x_{0},y_{0})$ 等价

> [!tip] 
> 不难看出，偏导数本质是一元函数

## 二元函数偏导数的几何意义

## 高阶偏导数

> [!tip]
> 求导作用于关系，不会改变自变量

### 二阶偏导数的定义

$$
\begin{align*}
\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \right) &= \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x^2} \quad \text{或} \quad f''_{xx}, \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \right) &= \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x\partial y} \quad \text{或} \quad f''_{xy}, \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \right) &= \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y\partial x} \quad \text{或} \quad f''_{yx}, \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \right) &= \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y^2} \quad \text{或} \quad f''_{yy}.
\end{align*}
$$

常称$\boldsymbol{\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x\partial y}}$、$\boldsymbol{\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y\partial x}}$为**混合偏导数**。

补充知识点：
当两个混合偏导数$f''_{xy}$和$f''_{yx}$在区域$D$内**连续**时，有$\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x\partial y} = \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y\partial x}$，即混合偏导数与求导次序无关。
