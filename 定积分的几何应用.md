# 定积分的几何应用

## 平面图形的面积

$$
S = \int_{a}^{b} \left[ f(x) - g(x) \right]  \, \mathrm{d}x 
$$

$$
S = \int_{\alpha}^{\beta}  \, \mathrm{d}\theta \int_{0}^{r(\theta)} r(\theta) \, \mathrm{d}r = \dfrac{1}{2} \int_{\alpha}^{\beta} r^{2}(\theta) \, \mathrm{d}\theta 
$$

## 旋转体体积

### 绕 $x$ 轴

$$
V_{x} = \pi \int_{a}^{b} f^{2}(x) \, \mathrm{d}x \qquad(\pi r^{2} h)
$$

### 绕 $y$ 轴

胶带模型

$$
V_{y} = 2 \pi \int_{a}^{b} x f(x) \, \mathrm{d}x \qquad(2 \pi r h)
$$

## 曲面积分

### 直角坐标形式

$$
S = \int_{L}^{} 2\pi f(x) \, \mathrm{d}s 
$$

其中

$\displaystyle \mathrm{d}s = \sqrt{ \mathrm{d}^{2}x + \mathrm{d}^{2}y } = \mathrm{d}x\sqrt{ 1 + f^{\prime 2}(x) }$

因此

$$
S = \int_{a}^{b} 2 \pi f(x) \sqrt{ 1 + f^{\prime 2}(x) } \, \mathrm{d}x 
$$

### 极坐标形式

$$
S = \int_{\alpha}^{\beta} 2 \pi r \sin\theta \, \mathrm{d}s 
$$

其中

$$
\begin{align}
\mathrm{d}s = & \sqrt{ \mathrm{d}^{2}x + \mathrm{d}^{2}y } \\
= & \sqrt{ (\mathrm{d} r\cos\theta)^{2} + (\mathrm{d} r \sin\theta)^{2} } \\
= & \sqrt{ 
    \begin{aligned}
    & \left[ r^{\prime}(\theta)\cos\theta - r(\theta)\sin\theta \right]^{2}+ \\
    & \left[ r^{\prime}(\theta)\sin\theta - r(\theta)\cos\theta \right]^{2} 
    \end{aligned} }
\mathrm{d}^{2}\theta \\
= & \sqrt{ 
    \begin{align}
     & r^{\prime 2}(\theta) \cos ^{2}(\theta) + r^{2}(\theta) \sin ^{2}(\theta) - \cancel{2r(\theta)r^{\prime}(\theta)\sin\theta \cos\theta} + \\
     & r^{\prime 2}(\theta) \sin ^{2}(\theta) + r^{2}(\theta) \cos ^{2}(\theta) + \cancel{2r(\theta)r^{\prime}(\theta)\sin\theta \cos\theta} 
    \end{align} \\
 } \mathrm{d}^{2}\theta \\
= & \sqrt{ r^{2}(\theta) + r^{\prime 2}(\theta) } \\
= & \sqrt{ r^{\prime 2} + r^{2} }
\end{align}
$$

因此

$$
S = \int_{\alpha}^{\beta} 2\pi r\sin \theta \sqrt{ r^{\prime 2} + r^{2} } \, \mathrm{d}\theta 
$$
