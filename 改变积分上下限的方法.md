# 改变积分上下限的方法

- $\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, \mathrm{d}x \to \int_{0}^{1} ? \, \mathrm{d}?$
    - $x=a+\mu(b-a),\mu \in (0,1)$
    - $\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{1} f(a+\mu(b-a)) \, \mathrm{d}(a+\mu(b-a))$
> [!note] 
> [[定积分的性质#积分中值定理^]]

- $\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, \mathrm{d}x \to \int_{a}^{b}  \, \mathrm{d}?$ (区间再现)
    - $x=a+b-u,a+u=a+b$
    - $x=a \to u=b;x=b \to u=a$
    - $\displaystyle I=\int_{b}^{a} f(a+b-u) \, \mathrm{d}(a+b-u)\to \mathrm{d}(-u)$
    - $\displaystyle \implies \int_{a}^{b} f(a+b-u) \, \mathrm{d}u$

- $\displaystyle \int_{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\pi} f(\sin x) \, \mathrm{d}x\to \int_{0}^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} ? \, \mathrm{d}?$
    - $x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}=u$
        - $I=\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} f\left[ \sin(u+\dfrac{\pi}{2}) \right] \, \mathrm{d}u$
        - $\displaystyle\quad=\int_{0}^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} f(\cos u) \, \mathrm{d}u$
    - $x+u=\pi\quad(2(0+\dfrac{\pi}{2}))$
        - $\displaystyle I=\int_{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}^{0} f\left[ \sin(\pi-u) \right] \, \mathrm{d}(-u)$
        - $\displaystyle \quad=\int_{0}^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} f\left[ \sin(\pi-u) \right] \, \mathrm{d}u$
