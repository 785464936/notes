# 一阶线性微分方程

$$
\boxed{?}y^{\prime}+p(x)y=q(x)
$$

猜测左侧为 $(uy)^{\prime} = uy^{\prime}+u^{\prime}y \to u^{\prime}(x) = u(x)p(x)$

$$
u(x)y^{\prime}(x)+u(x)p(x)y(x)=u(x)q(x)
$$

$\dfrac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d}x}=u(x)p(x) \implies \dfrac{1}{u(x)}\mathrm{d}u=p(x)\mathrm{d}x$

两侧同时积分 $\displaystyle \ln u = \int p(x) \, \mathrm{d}x \implies \boxed{u=e^{\int p(x)\mathrm{d}x}}$

代入上式 $\left[ u(x)y(x) \right]^{\prime}=u(x)q(x)$，$u(x)y(x)$ 记为 $M$

$$
\dfrac{\mathrm{d}M}{\mathrm{d}x}=u(x)q(x)
$$

$$
M = u(x)y(x) = \int u(x)q(x) \, \mathrm{d}x + C
$$

$$
y=\underbrace{ e^{-\int p(x) \, \mathrm{d}x} }_{ \dfrac{1}{u(x)} }\left[ q(x)\underbrace{ e^{\int p(x) \, \mathrm{d}x} }_{ u(x) } +C \right] 
$$
