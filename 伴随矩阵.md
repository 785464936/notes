# 伴随矩阵

$A = (a_{ij})_{n \times n}\quad A^{*} = (a_{ji})_{n \times n}$

二阶伴随矩阵速记

主对调，副变号

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}^* = 
\begin{bmatrix}
d & -b \\
-c & a
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### 伴随矩阵相关公式

- $AA^* = A^*A = |A|E$
- $|A^*| = |A|^{n-1} \quad |A| = 0 \implies |A^*| = 0$
- $(AB)^* = B^*A^*$
- $(A^{\mathrm{T}})^* = (A^*)^{\mathrm{T}}$
