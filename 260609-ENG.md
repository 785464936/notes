Here’s a common scenario that any number of [[entrepreneur#entrepreneurs|entrepreneurs]] face today: you’re the [[ceo#CEO|CEO]] of a small business, and though you’re [[phrases#making a nice profit|making a nice profit]], you need to find a way to [[phrases#take it to the next level|take it to the next level]]. And, [[despite#despite|despite]] improvements to the [[premium#premium|premium]], paid version, few people were making the [[upgrade#upgrade|upgrade]].
